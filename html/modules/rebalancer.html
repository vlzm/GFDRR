gbp.rebalancer¶

Warning

Early prototype — will be redesigned in a future phase.

Bike-sharing rebalancing solver built on OR-Tools. Formulates the problem as a Pickup and Delivery Problem (PDP) and solves it with a vehicle routing engine.
